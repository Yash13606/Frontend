## 🔍 Problem Statement

Businesses across retail, fashion, food & beverage, logistics, and hospitality face four compounding operational crises — all currently addressed by fragmented, expensive, or passive tools:

| Problem | Description |
|---|---|
| 📦 Retail & Business Losses | Shoplifting surged 93% between 2019–2023. Traditional CCTV is passive — it records incidents after they happen but does nothing to prevent them. |
| 👀 Zero Customer Intelligence | Businesses have no data on where customers spend time, which products attract attention, or what drives purchase decisions in-store. |
| 🧑‍💼 Unmonitored Staff Performance | Managers cannot track whether staff are fulfilling tasks, staying in assigned zones, or spending time unproductively — especially in large stores and warehouses. |
| 🔥 Fire & Safety Risk | Conventional fire alarms react too late, fail in open/large spaces, and cannot distinguish real fire from cigarette smoke or steam — wasting emergency response time. |

## 💡 Our Solution

A single, fully integrated AI-camera platform that replaces five separate tools — delivering complete business intelligence, loss prevention, staff accountability, and safety in **one package** sold to enterprises.

| Module | Description |

## 🖥️ Technology Architecture

| Tech Layer | Details |
|---|---|
| Computer Vision | YOLOv8 / Vision Transformers for real-time object & person detection at edge (no cloud latency for critical alerts) |
| Behavioral AI | LSTM/GRU-based gesture and movement sequence modeling for theft, idleness, and fire behavior classification |
| Edge Processing | On-device NVIDIA Jetson / Hailo AI chips — no raw video sent to cloud; only alert metadata synced (privacy-first) |
| Fire Detection Model | Multi-spectral CNN trained on fire, smoke, steam, dust, and cigarette datasets — 97%+ accuracy in distinguishing real fire |
| Dashboard & Alerts | Real-time web + mobile dashboard; WhatsApp/SMS/email alert pipeline; POS/ERP integration via REST API |
| Data Privacy | All person embeddings are anonymized; no biometric database required for re-ID; GDPR & IT Act 2000-compliant architecture |
